Solar Direct Cooler

A solar-powered cooling system designed to provide affordable, resilient, and energy-efficient cooling without relying on large battery storage.

Our vision is simple: make clean cooling affordable, reliable, and accessible for people living in hot climates and weak-grid environments.

How It Works

During the day, solar energy freezes water into ice. At night, a small pump moves air through copper tubing in the ice to deliver cool air indoors.

Day — Store cooling

Solar PV panels directly power a DC compressor that freezes water into ice inside an insulated tank. Ice acts as a thermal battery.

Night — Deliver cooling

A low-power DC air pump moves air through copper tubing embedded in the ice. Cooled air is delivered into the living space.

Why Ice Instead of Batteries?

Unlike traditional solar air conditioning that depends heavily on expensive lithium batteries, Solar Direct Cooler stores energy as ice.

Conventional solar AC

Large lithium battery bank to run the compressor when sun is low. High upfront cost, limited cycle life, and charge/discharge losses.

Solar Direct Cooler

Ice as phase-change thermal storage. Small battery for pump and controls only. Direct solar-to-compressor coupling. Lower cost and simpler maintenance.
